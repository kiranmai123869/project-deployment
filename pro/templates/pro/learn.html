Body Part Treatment Specialists

Kidney (Nephrology)

Nephrologists specialize in treating kidney-related diseases.

Kids (Pediatrics)

Pediatricians focus on the health and well-being of children.

Heart (Cardiology)

Cardiologists diagnose and treat heart diseases.

Lungs (Pulmonology)

Pulmonologists manage respiratory system diseases.

Digestive System (Gastroenterology)

Gastroenterologists treat digestive disorders.

Orthopedic (Bones & Joints)

Orthopedic surgeons handle bones and joint issues.

Skin (Dermatology)

Dermatologists treat skin, hair, and nail conditions.
